Tomato Basil Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a brief description of the pasta, its good and what not. Trust me this is the best pasta you will ever eat

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • High-quality tomato basil pre-made pasta sauce, make sure to hide this from your guests and pretend you made it yourself.
  • A pot
  • Water
  • A pinch of salt
  • A very nice looking plate
  • A pasta strainer

Steps

  1. Add pasta to boiling water and cook 10-13 minutes, until desired consistency is reached.
  2. Add a pinch of salt to pasta.
  3. Stir pot very casually and at random times looking determined.
  4. Strain pasta and place it in the very nice plate
  5. Add a generous amount of pasta sauce
  6. Enjoy!
