Tito's Steak Marinade

Description

This is a brief description of the steak, its good and what not. Trust me this is the best steak you will ever eat

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of high-quality marinade, from the hills of Peru, or something
  • 3 gallons of imported limes from stafrombilous beater
  • A pinch of essence of pure joy
  • Butter, quantity, as much as you like
  • 2 tbs of high expectations
  • A steak

Steps

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet until the metal is soft and red
  2. Reshape heated skillet to perfectly mimic the shape of your steak
  3. Throw away the skillet, trust me the skillet was not the right shape
  4. Heat a new skillet to a reasonable temperature
  5. Place a glob of butter in the skillet, careful not to burn the butter. Being a professional chef is not required but highly suggested here
  6. Gently fling the steak into the skillet from a minium of 3ft away
  7. Add a generous amount of marinade, you know that you are done when the steak is no longer visible
  8. Enjoy!
